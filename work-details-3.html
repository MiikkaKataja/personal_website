B2B go-to-market for Spark Sustainability

Planning and implementing a b2b go-to-market process for a climate education app

Spark Sustainability, a climate competence & culture solution for sustainable organisations with 16k b2c users, needed support in understanding the b2b market, their target customers and setting up b2b sales-led-growth efforts. Over 6 months, we worked with the founding team to understand their Ideal Customer Profiles (ICP’s), as well as conceptualize and operationalize a b2b sales process to acquire first paying customers, and speed up their pivot to b2b markets.

With our support, the founding team of Spark Sustainability understood the difference between b2c and b2b markets, identified target customers, and fine-tuned a proposition that creates value for the new customers. The team was able to establish a repeatable b2b sales process and acquire first paying customers, doubling their revenue in 2022.

How did we achieve this?

We started by creating hypotheses for an ideal customer profile, a b2b value proposition, price point, and sales process for the previously unknown b2b market segment. A key outcome of this process was a clear description of Spark Sustainability’s ICP, which guided future sales & marketing activities. Based on the ICP description, we started a digital outbound sales campaign using LinkedIn and email automation tools, contacting 250 target customer decision makers, resulting in 50 qualified sales meetings with target customers (25% conversion from outbound contact to meetings). In the meetings, we supported the founding team in testing the hypothesised ICP, value proposition and price point. We documented learnings from the meetings, and completed several iterations on each of the elements.

  • Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) definition
  • B2B sales playbook plan and execution
  • Commercial proposition & business model design
