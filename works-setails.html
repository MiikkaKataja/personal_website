Product discovery for Epiprodux

Validating value of a new customer segment for a B2B SaaS startup

Epiprodux, a team working on a solution for Product Managers, was looking for growth through understanding new user groups and customer segments for their SaaS product. To accelerate this process, we did a qualitative customer research stint for the CEO, including customer interviews, market segmentation analysis and a recommendation for next steps over the course of 3 weeks.

By working with us, the Epiprodux founding team was able to accelerate their product & customer discovery process with several weeks. The findings of our customer interviews resulted in further validation of the new large enterprise segment, and the team decided to pursue a market entry to the segment.

How did we achieve this?

The collaboration kicked off by reviewing existing Ideal Customer Profiles (ICP’s) of Epiprodux, and creating hypotheses about a new user group where the Epiprodux team saw new potential: Product Managers in large enterprises. Our objective was to increase understanding about the new user group, and validate business potential of this group for Epiprodux. We started by setting up a customer interview plan, with an aim to test the hypotheses made earlier, and understand the user’s jobs and pains better. We ran 8 customer interviews with selected representatives of the new user group and concluded the findings in richer ICP definitions and a several different frameworks for customer segmentation.

  • Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) definition
  • Customer interviews
  • Synthetisation of results
